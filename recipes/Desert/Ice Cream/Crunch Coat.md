Joe's Awesome Crunch Coat
=================================================


Ingredients
--------------------------------------
* ½ cup Heath bit
* ¾ cup sprinkles
* 2 crushed sugar cone (Heath bit size)
* ½ cup peanuts
* 2 Tbsp. of sugar

Directions
-----------------------------------------------

1. Chop peanuts in to small pieces.
2. Cook them in skillet with sugar till caramelized. let cool on plate or foil.
3. Mix all together.
4. Add lots to ice cream. enjoy
