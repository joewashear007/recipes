Fried Bread & Sauce
=====================================================

Ingredients
------------------------------------------
###Bread:

Large Batch          | Small Batch
---------------------|--------------------
5 lbs. flour         | 5 c. flour
4 c. warm water      | 2 c. warm water
fist full of salt    | 1 teaspoon salt
3 yeast              | 2 dry yeast
oil in warm water    | little oil in water

###Sauce:
* oil
* fresh garlic
* celery
* onion
* green pepper
* hot pepper
* salt and pepper
* tomato paste
* Italian seasoning
* pinch of baking soda
* parsley flakes


Directions
--------------------------------------------------
###Bread:

1. Make a well in flour, Knead,
2. put oil on bread.
3. Let rise 30-45 minutes.
4. Roll out, poke with fork.
5. Fry in hot oil.


###Sauce:
1. Fry in oil:
    * fresh garlic
    * celery
    * onion
    * green pepper
    * hot pepper
    * salt & pepper

2. Add tomato paste and water
3. Add Italian seasoning. pinch of baking soda, parsley flakes
